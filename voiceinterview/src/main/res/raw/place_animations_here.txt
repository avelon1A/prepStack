RIVE ANIMATION FILES LOCATION
=============================

Place your .riv animation files in this directory.

Example:
- voice_animation.riv
- microphone_animation.riv
- sound_wave.riv

Download free Rive animations from:
https://rive.app/community/

Or create your own at:
https://rive.app/editor

After adding your .riv file:
1. Sync Gradle
2. Reference it in code as: R.raw.voice_animation
3. Use VoiceRiveAnimation composable

See RIVE_SETUP.md for complete instructions.
